Cybercrime Case Study:

One common example of cybercrime is phishing. In phishing attacks, cybercriminals send fake emails or messages that appear to be from trusted sources such as banks or companies. .

Many people fall victim to phishing because the messages look genuine. Once the attacker gets access to personal information, they can misuse it for financial fraud or identity theft.

This case highlights the importance of being cautious while interacting online and verifying the authenticity of messages before sharing any personal information.
